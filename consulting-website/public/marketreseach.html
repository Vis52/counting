Detailed Program Syllabus

Below is the complete curriculum for our flagship Market Research Beginner Certification

Module 1: Market Research Fundamentals

+
  • Introduction to market research 2 hours
  • Types of research: Primary vs. Secondary 2 hours
  • Research process overview 2 hours
  • Case study: Market research for a product launch 2 hours
  • Assignment: Define a research objective 2 hours

Module 2: Survey Design

+
  • Principles of survey design 2 hours
  • Question types and formats 2 hours
  • Avoiding bias in surveys 2 hours
  • Case study: Consumer survey analysis 2 hours
  • Assignment: Create a survey questionnaire 2 hours

Module 3: Data Collection

+
  • Data collection methods 2 hours
  • Sampling techniques 2 hours
  • Online survey tools 2 hours
  • Case study: Data collection for a retail brand 2 hours
  • Assignment: Collect sample data 2 hours

Module 4: Data Analysis Basics

+
  • Introduction to data analysis 2 hours
  • Using Excel for analysis 2 hours
  • Descriptive statistics 2 hours
  • Case study: Analyzing survey results 2 hours
  • Assignment: Analyze a dataset 2 hours

Module 5: Reporting and Presentation

+
  • Structuring research reports 2 hours
  • Data visualization basics 2 hours
  • Presenting findings to stakeholders 2 hours
  • Case study: Research report for a client 2 hours
  • Assignment: Create a research report 2 hours

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this program designed for?

+

Our Market Research Beginner program is designed for:

  • Complete beginners with no research experience
  • Career changers entering market research
  • Professionals adding research skills
  • Entrepreneurs conducting their own market studies
  • Students preparing for research careers

No prior research knowledge is required.

What is the time commitment required?

+

The 15-day Beginner program requires approximately 4-5 hours per day:

  • 2 hours of live sessions (recordings available)
  • 1-2 hours of video tutorials and readings
  • 1-2 hours for projects and assignments

Designed for intensive learning over a short period.

Recognized by employers worldwide.
