Who is this program designed for?

+

Our E-Commerce Essentials program is designed for:

  • Small business owners moving to e-commerce
  • Professionals seeking digital marketing skills
  • Freelancers offering e-commerce services
  • Students exploring online business careers

No prior business experience is required.

+

The 8-week Essentials program requires approximately 8-10 hours per week:
