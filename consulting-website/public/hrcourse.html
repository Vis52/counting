Below is the complete curriculum for our flagship HR Beginner Certification

+
  • Introduction to human resources 2 hours

  • Basic HR policies and procedures 3 hours
  • Case study: HR in a small business 3 hours
  • Assignment: Draft an HR policy 4 hours

Module 2: Recruitment Basics

+
  • Job analysis and descriptions 4 hours
  • Sourcing and screening candidates 3 hours
  • Interview techniques 2 hours
  • Case study: Recruitment process 3 hours
  • Assignment: Create a job posting 5 hours

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this program designed for?

+

Our HR Beginner program is designed for:

  • Complete beginners with no HR experience
  • Career changers entering HR
  • Professionals adding HR skills
  • Entrepreneurs managing their own teams
  • Students preparing for HR careers

No prior HR knowledge is required.

What is the time commitment required?

+

The 8-week Beginner program requires approximately 8-10 hours per week:

  • 3-4 hours of live sessions (recordings available)
  • 2-3 hours of video tutorials and readings
  • 3-4 hours for projects and assignments

Designed to fit around full-time work with proper planning.

Recognized by employers worldwide.
