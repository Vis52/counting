Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this program designed for?

+

Our Business Strategy Foundations program is designed for:

  • Mid-career professionals transitioning to strategy roles
  • Managers and team leaders involved in strategic planning
  • Entrepreneurs and business owners developing growth strategies
  • Consultants looking to strengthen their strategy toolkit
  • Recent graduates targeting strategy positions

The program assumes basic business knowledge but no prior strategy experience is required.

What is the time commitment required?

+

The 8-week Foundations program requires approximately 8-10 hours per week:

  • 3-4 hours of live sessions (recordings available)
  • 2-3 hours of readings and video lessons
  • 3-4 hours for assignments and project work

The program is designed to be manageable alongside full-time work with proper time management.

What kind of certification will I receive?

+

Upon successful completion (80%+ overall score), you'll receive:

  • Digital Business Strategy Foundations Certification from StrategicEdge
  • Verified digital badge to display on LinkedIn and resumes
  • PDF certificate with unique verification code
  • Transcript of completed modules and grades

Our certification is recognized by employers worldwide and can be verified through our website.

What are the technical requirements?

+

You'll need:

  • Reliable internet connection (minimum 5Mbps recommended)
  • Computer with webcam and microphone for live sessions
  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari)
  • Microsoft Office or equivalent for assignments
  • Zoom installed for virtual sessions

No specialized software is required beyond these basics.
